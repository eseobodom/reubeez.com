WHAT IS FENUGREEK?

Fenugreek
Fenugreek Description

Fenugreek is a clover-like herb native to the Mediterranean region, southern Europe, and western Asia. Its seeds, which smell and taste like maple syrup, have been used in cooking and as medicine. Fenugreek is used as an ingredient in spice blends and a flavoring agent in foods, beverages, and tobacco

HOW TO USE

- Fenugreek seeds can be ground into a powder and added to curries, salads, smoothies, or other dishes for a flavorful boost and potential health benefits.
- To enjoy fenugreek as a tea, steep 2 teaspoons of seeds in hot water for 5-10 minutes, then strain and enjoy.
